"I want to study",Says Little Girl With A Big Dream.Her little inspiration story.

The little Girls name is Rupa.Her mother's name is Mst.Rani and her father's name is Md.Hashinur.Rupa was born in 12.07.2011.She comes from a small village of Parbatipur in Bangladesh.Due to family issues in 2015 her family moved to Saidpur,When she was only five years old.She has five members in her family.She reads in class three.Rupa appears to be just like any onther nine years old girl.She is sweet,with bright,dark eyes and a tender smile.She is occasioanlly cause for small mischief and play some tricks as all other kids do.Her young mind is wired to learning.She soaks up new information like a spange,and she is open to new discoveries.She loves every minutes what she spends in my coaching classes.

"I enjoy the moments when I am with my friends in the class-room,also when we are in playground.We play several games,which games me bundles of joy"-Says Rupa

Rupa with her younger sister
In 2019,somehow her family heared about my coaching center.They brought her to admitted her in my coaching center.Since her first day,I have never separated from her.She is a wonderful and intelligent child.She can do many things independently.Yet I feel I should be beside her all the time.Since childhood,She has faced lots of major family issues.She saw her father beating her mother badly.She saw domestic violence only at the age of three.But the great thing is,She never let all this affect her.She is a well focused child.She never miss her classes.She is always active in her class and she always prepare her lessons before class.
Rupa with her family
Rupa story shows how important the efforts we make towards creating a better future for all our children are.Our DoPeace.org believe in the dreams of every boy and girl,who are stucking in poverty.Our organization invest in their education and development.There is still a lot of work ahead of us to ensure that these lovely children have the best possible conditions to grow up,learn,play,develop their potential and enjoy the happy and normal childhood they deserve.
